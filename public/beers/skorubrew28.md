Going for a basic IPA but with some brown malt

Grain Bill
-----

900g pilsner malt
100g aurora malt
100g brown malt
100g acid malt


Hop Schedule
-------------

* 2g amarillo @ 60 minutes
* 3g amarillo @ 30 minutes
* 3g amarillo @ 10 minutes
* 5g amarillo dry hop


Method
-------

* Batch sparge with water 70C
* Bring to boil and leave for 1 hour
